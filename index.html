things i'm building

  • Ebook Your Friends - A Python framework to make Twitter bots based on the content of your timeline. Includes a Python script to run your bot, a Markov text generator for making tweets that deals with @ mentions and hashtags, and a bash script for setting up your bot on Heroku.
  • Markov Text Generator - A more general-use Markov text generator that trains itself on a source text to create output text, from short sentences to novellas, based on the probability a word follows a sequence of words. Pip installable.
  • Noun Shuffle - Shuffles the nouns in whatever text you give it. Especially fun with philosophy and poetry! Built with Python, Flask, and Heroku. Source.
  • Feelings.fm - Tell the app how you're doing and it creates a playlist for you with songs related to your feelings. Uses the Spotify and Echo Nest APIs, TextBlob for sentiment analysis, and built with Python, Flask, and Heroku. Source.
  • Anna in Wonderland - Put your friends in classic works of literature: the script identifies people entities in a source text, then swaps them with people of your choice to create a new story with a character cast of your choice. Submitted to National Novel Generating Month 2014. Responsible for Pride and Prejudice and Hacker School.
  • slayer.ly - A URL “shortener” that creates new URLs out of Slayer song titles that are way more metal than your long, boring URLs. Built with Python, Flask, and sqlite3.
